    Ford Fiesta

    The Ford Fiesta is in its final year after a long good run; and if you count its years in Europe before it came to the U.S., that run has been longer and better. The Fiesta is a subcompact sedan and hatchback with a choice of three engines, from perky and inexpensive, to tiny and unique, to pocket-rocket responsive.

    Ford Aspire

    The Ford Aspire launched in 1994 as a replacement to the Festiva, which was one of the most fuel-efficient Fords ever made. This subcompact car was a budget-friendly option to those looking for something basic. The Aspire came equipped with a small 1.3L four-cylinder engine that output 64 horsepower. While it won't raise your pulse behind the wheel, this Ford was fuel efficient, achieving up to 42 mpg on the highway.
